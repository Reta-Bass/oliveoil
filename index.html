Mechanism

Olive oil contains very important fatty acids and vitamins. It also contains ω3 in low percentage. Adding long chain ω3 fatty acids is produced a very nutritional mixture . Thus it is the phospholipids and more particularly phosphatidylserine that lead the fatty acids in the cellular membrane . Phospholypids combined with fatty acids penetrate the membrane and strengthen the outer layer or the inner layer of membrane, contributing in the improvement of resilience and elasticity of cellular membrane.
